## **Manual\_ShadowsofAlmia**



For use with Manual Archipelago Client. (https://discord.gg/T5bcsVHByx)





#### What do you need

* A completed save file for Shadows of Almia (DLC optional)

* Manual Archipelago Client





#### Choose your Goal



* Collect the Gems and Capture Darkai at Altru Tower
* Capture the Regi-trio and Capture Regigigas in Hippowdon Temple
* Capture both Darkrai \& Regigigas
* Capture all the Legendary Pokemon hidden in the Almia Region





### Starting Location



Either Randomize your Starting Location or Choose between 11 different ones.





### What are the Locations



Every Pokemon capture is a Location.





### What is Randomized



* 31 Permits: You need this before you are allowed to capture anything in an Area.
* 16 Fly Points: You can't land anywhere without them.
* 25 Field Moves: You need the item before you are allowed to use any Field Moves.
* 17 Partner Pokemon: Which of the Partner Pokemon are allowed to accompany you. (You are always allowed to use your Partner's Assist in battle)
* 18 Poke Assists: Required before you are allowed to use a captured Pokemon's Assist in Battle.
* 5 Boat Keys and Wailord: You need the key before you are allowed to use the corresponding boat.
* 5 Gems: Required before you are allowed to capture Darkrai.
* 6 Missions and Ranger Net: If you decide to include them in the options.





### Special Requirements



* Every floor of the Capture Arena needs the next progressive permit.
* Hippowdon requires Agility and Doduo.
* Regirock requires Crush and Garchomp.
* Regice requires Burn and Magmortar.
* Registeel requires Cut and Gallade.
* Regigigas requires Regirock, Regice and Registeel.
* Darkrai requires Red Gem, Blue Gem, Yellow Gem and 2 Progressive Crystals.
